Grow Timeline

  1. 2025-08-01 — Germinated

    Seed split and taproot visible. Placed into damp paper towel and moved to 72°F cabinet.

  2. 2025-08-07 — Seedling

    First true leaves appeared. Transplanted to 2" starter plug, gentle misting twice daily.

  3. 2025-09-03 — Vegetative

    Moved to 3-gallon pot; 18/6 light schedule. Feeding with mild veg nutrient at 200ppm.

  4. 2025-10-01 — Pre-flower

    Noticed pistils and stretch beginning; switched to 12/12 to induce flowering.

  5. 2025-10-27 — Flowering (Week 5)

    Trichomes milky on upper buds; lowered humidity to 45% and increased potassium-focused feed.
